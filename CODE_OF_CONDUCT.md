# Code of Conduct

MGB64 is a preservation and reverse-engineering project. Keep discussion
technical, lawful, and constructive.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful of other contributors' time and experience.
- Review code and claims critically without making it personal.
- Keep legal boundaries clear: do not request, share, or link ROMs or extracted
  copyrighted assets.
- Prefer small, reproducible bug reports and focused pull requests.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, slurs, or discriminatory remarks.
- Personal attacks, sustained bad-faith argument, or derailing technical threads.
- Sharing copyrighted game data, asking others to provide it, or helping bypass
  the project's asset-free rules.
- Publishing private contact information or confidential reports.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or pull requests;
or block participants when needed to keep the project healthy and legally safe.
Reports can be made through GitHub issues, discussions, or private maintainer
contact when available. Do not include ROMs, extracted assets, or screenshots
containing copyrighted game imagery in a conduct report.
